The Club

Silverback Taekwondo started off as an idea and an aspiration and materialised in January 2016. The name ‘Silverback’ was suggested by one of the students and it was chosen for its symbolic attributes which include dignity, strength, compassion, responsibility, leadership and loyalty. These attributes are some of the core principles of Silverback Taekwondo and form the foundation on which our students will strive towards their goals.

We currently hold three classes, one in Tower Hamlets and two in Barking. We are a friendly and welcoming club and encourage adults and children of all ages from 6 years onwards to come and give Taekwondo a try.

Our qualified instructors are CRB/DBS checked and are authorised instructors affiliated to the England Taekwondo Association (ETA) and the International Taekwondo Federation (ITF).

Our Mission Statement

Through Taekwondo training, Silverback strive to develop the full potential in our students, enabling them to embrace the challenges of life more successfully, while setting the highest standard of excellence.

We set our Taekwondo programme to benefit all our students and help strengthen their body and their mind through:

  • Physical training
  • Building self-esteem
  • Building confidence
We believe that stronger minds and bodies will help individuals better their own lives and improve the world around them.

At Silverback Taekwondo you will be encouraged to reach your full potential in a supportive and friendly manner. All students shall be treated equally, regardless of gender, race, age or ability.
